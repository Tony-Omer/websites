Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

for the dough:

  • 2/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated suger
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brouwn suger
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter
